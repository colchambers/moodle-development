Welcome

It's a pleasure to welcome you to the second presentation of H807. Take a look around the website and familiarise yourself with the structure of the course. You will see a variety of links, which take you to various parts of the course. We suggest you start with the Course Guide, which you will find at the very top of the website structure. Spend a little time getting to know where you can find things on the website, such as links to the OU blogging system and instructions and the course wiki. StudentHome is another area of useful information. This is where you find details on how to use the FirstClass forums. The forums area will take you to individual tutor groups, and to the cafe, which is a great place to meet students from across the whole course. The course spans 22 weeks and consists of three blocks of learning activities. Initially, you will be able to see only the first block of material. As the weeks roll by, more of the course will become visible. Each block has an introduction, and the course material is structured as learning activities with tasks for you to complete, sometimes individually and sometimes with other members of your group.
