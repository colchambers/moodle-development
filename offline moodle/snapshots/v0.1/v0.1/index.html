Quick Start

If you just want to get started goto Offline Moodle home page. The suggested process to follow is :

  • Step 1: View the course
  • Step 2: Click 'Update/Install Course'. Opens installed coureses page.
  • Step 3: Click course name to update. Opens window to OU VLE content download page
  • Step 4: Click 'Download your latest asset bundle'. Begins asset bundle download.
  • Step 5: Click 'Install asset bundle'. Opens page to locate and install asset bundle.
  • Step 6: Click 'Install asset bundle' button. Show course that has been updated/installed.

Background

Web development is a process of turning the clients requests into reality. There is considerable room for interpretation of customer requirements and therefore we aim to provide our clients with methods whereby they can sample our interpretation and interact with it as much as possible.

This demonstration package has been provided as an example of phase one of the Offline Moodle System we aim to provide. The pages within the demonstration package have been designed with sufficient functionality to provide a demonstration of key deliverables and to serve as a clear example of what has been agreed. You will therefore find that some functionality such as menus and links do not work as expected or at all. You may also find that lists of documents commonly link to the same document to ease administration of the demonstration. If you would like to discuss the functionality that will be provided in the final version then please contact us.

Phase one of the Offline Moodle System consists of two main sections:
  • Offline Moodle: Update Management Interface
  • OU VLE: Update Management Interface
Demonstrations of both sections have been included in this release. As well as providing a demonstration of the interfaces, we have provided short explanations of some of the key features.
