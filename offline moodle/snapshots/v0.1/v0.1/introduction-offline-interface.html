Version 0.1: Offline Moodle Interface

A demonstration version of the Offline Moodle Interface has been created to provide a clear indication of the functionality we intend to provide. The current version is in the process of being agreed with The Open University in principle and will be developed over the next coming versions with further functionality developed.

For the purposes of this demonstration we have focused on developing the following core functionality which will be available to all pages within the Update Management component:
  • the intended look and feel.
  • the intended navigational elements.
  • examples of key pages and sections including; generating and retrieving asset bundles.

With this release we have focused on providing examples of wizards to ease the course update and install process summarise.

Starting point

The demonstration begins in Offline Moodle viewing the home page.

Procedure

The demonstration begins with the Moodle home page and shows how the update management component will be reached by a link from a Moodle Block on the right hand side.
