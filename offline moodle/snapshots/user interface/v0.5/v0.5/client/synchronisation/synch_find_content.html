Introduction

In order to synchronise content you must first find the item you wish to synchronise. This page allows you to browse Moodle content for items to synchronise and, if you wish, add these to your list of saved shortcuts.

Content

Explore content by clicking the to load new content under the selected item. Click the icon to hide content.

When you have found the item you wish to synchronise, click 'Synchronise' to being synchronising the content or add it to your shortcuts for quick synchronising of this item in the future.
